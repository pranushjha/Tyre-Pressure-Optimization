Why Tire Optimization is Important

🚚 Fleet Safety

Incorrect tire pressure is one of the leading causes of truck accidents worldwide. Proper tire pressure ensures stability, braking efficiency and safer transportation.

⛽ Fuel Efficiency

Underinflated tires increase rolling resistance which leads to higher fuel consumption. Optimized tire pressure can improve fuel efficiency by up to 5%.

💰 Reduced Maintenance

Monitoring tire wear and pressure prevents early tire damage and reduces fleet maintenance costs.

🤖 AI Driven Monitoring

Using Machine Learning models allows fleet managers to predict tire risks and optimize operations automatically.
